FACTORS THAT INFLUENCE DRUG EFFECTS

  • Dosage-this is the quantity or amount of drugs taken. The greater the dose the greater the effect
  • Solubility- this is the ability of a drug to be dissolved, they are in two categories
    •        •      Water Soluble Drugs- this are drugs that dissolve in water only and do not reach cells of a nervous system
           •      Fat Soluble Drugs-these are drugs that dissolve into the fat tissues of the body and affect the central nervous system for a day or weeks
  • Interaction-drugs may interact with other drugs in the body
