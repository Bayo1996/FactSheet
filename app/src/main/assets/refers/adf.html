Discuss medication adherence at each appointment with your health care provider.

Tell your health care provider if you’re having difficulty following your regimen.

Don’t forget to mention any side effects you’re having.

Side effects from HIV medicines are a major reason why medication adherence can be difficult.

Let your health care provider know if your regimen is too complicated to follow.

Your health care provider may simplify your regimen by including fewer HIV medicines or by reducing the number of times a day you need to take your HIV medicines.

Discuss any issues that are causing you to skip medicines.

Your health care provider can recommend resources to help you deal with the issues.
