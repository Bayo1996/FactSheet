DEFINATION

  1. A drug is a substance that interacts with a living and thereby brings about changes in the way the organism functions
  2. Broadly speaking, is any substance that alters normal bodily function. there is no single, precise definition
  3. In pharmacology defines a drug as a chemical substance used in treatment, cure prevention, or diagnosis of disease or used to otherwise enhance physical or mental well being. drugs may be prescribed for limited duration, or on a regular basis for chronic disorders
  4. In the United States, the federal food, drug and cosmetic Act: defines drug as articles intended for use in the diagnosis,cure,mitigation,treatment,or prevention of disease in man or other animals and articles(other then food) intended to affect the structure or nay function of the body of man or animals

Depending on the nature of the effects can either be used as poisons or medicine or not drugs at all.
