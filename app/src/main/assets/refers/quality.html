Quality Policy Statement

Moi Teaching and Referral Hospital (MTRH) strives to be leading multi-specialty hospital for Healthcare, Training and Research in Africa. In pursuit of this, the hospital committed to providing timely, cost effective and patient-centered specialized healthcare services; fostering learning and growth through training and research, utilization of new technologies, continuous improvement and participation in National Health Planning.

The Board, Management and staff of Moi Teaching and Referral Hospital are committed to effective implementation and continual improvement of Quality Management System (QMS) that complies with ISO 9001:2015 standard requirements.

Through achievement of set Annual Performance Contract Targets, Work Plan and Quality Objectives that are reviewed regularly, MTRH endeavours to consistently meet and/or exceed its customers’ and other interested parties’ needs and expectations while complying with applicable Statutory and Regulatory requirements.

Quality Objectives

In order to meet tenets enshrined in this Quality Policy, the Department has set for itself the following Quality Objectives.

  1. To determine customer satisfaction level by developing a questionnaire tool by the third quarter at Rafiki Centre of Excellence in Adolescent Health.
  2. To improve viral load suppression rate from 74% to 90% by June 2018 through suppression group meetings and peer mentor sessions.
