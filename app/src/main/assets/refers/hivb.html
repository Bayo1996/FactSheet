WINDOW PERIOD

  • After HIV infection there is a delay before HIV antibodies become detectable in the blood ( 1-3) weeks)
  • Rapid or Elisa tests will be negative
  • Latest Elisa tests can detect infection at 2 weeks
  • PCR can detect infection at approximately two weeks after infection
  • This is an important point to remember when dealing with a negative test- window period must be excluded before confirming the result as negative
