DRUG INTERACTION

  • Independent-drugs taken to work independently of each other. Neither one affects the action of the other i.e. miraa or beer
  • Antagonistic-drugs that when taken together interact the effect of either or both is blocked or reduce; this interaction can be seen in the equation 2+2=0 or 2+2=2 i. e cigarettes+bhang, miraa+cigarettes.
  • Synergistic-the interaction of two or more drugs resulting in an effect greater than an addictive effect. It occurs when one drug increases the potency of another by interfering with its disposition, thus increasing the effect of a given dose. This can be seen in this equation 2+2=4.
