MISSION

The AMPATH Adolescent Care Program is committed to providing holistic care to adolescents by providing both preventive and curative services. The program is dedicated to youth friendly service provision and will foster collaborative effort aimed at improving the knowledge and skills of the staff on adolescent health, research and use of technology. It will ensure adequate participation of the adolescents in matters concerning their health.

VISION

To be the leading program in Africa offering holistic care to adolescents.

MOTTO

Excellence in health care provision to adolescents of all walks of life.

ORGANISATION’S DIRECTION

We determine our current organization by assessing our Strengths, weakness, opportunities and weakness (SWOT).

CORE VALUES

  • Excellence in care provision –Excellence in patient care, education and innovation

  • Respect for privacy of the clients- Respect for all patients , families, and community members and colleagues from all backgrounds

  • Integrity-abiding by the highest moral standards while working clients and the core team.
  • Innovation – using the resources at our disposal to innovate solutions to challenges experienced by adolescents in accessing and utilizing the health services
  • None judgmental approach to care- we serve adolescents from all walks of life. We use the human rights approach in increasing their access to health services.
  • Engagement of the youth at all levels – Rafiki Center is by the adolescents for the adolescents
  • Collaboration with like-minded partners – uniting a multidisciplinary team including youth with the best care
