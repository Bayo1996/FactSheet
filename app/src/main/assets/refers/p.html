DRUGS AND SUBSTANCE USE AND ABUSE

DRUG DEPENDENCE

This is a state of psychological and physical need of a drug and anyone who uses any kind of psychoactive drugs ahs the potential to develop chemical dependence (physical need)

  • Psychological Dependence-this is a strong desire to repeat the use of a drug for emotional reasons

  • Physical Dependence- this is a condition in which presence of a drug becomes normal and necessary for the clients to continue functioning in the body and if not taken it alters the normal functioning of the body and become harmful for the user to discontinue the drug once abused

  • Tolerance-this is state of physical adaptation where larger and larger doses are administered to produce the desired effects and this can be rapid or gradual
    •      o its a state of drug dependence that supersedes dependence
         o its a state of being less sensitive to drugs
         o it reduces sensitivity or increases resistance to the drugs

  • Cross Tolerance- this is using one drug to counteract the effects of the other one

  • Withdrawal- this is condition where the use of a drug to physical addicted is discontinued, however the victim may suffer withdrawal sickness symptoms and signs include loss of appetite, inability to sleep,vomiting,anxiety and depressions depending on the type of drug and length and time

  • Drug Addiction- this is a state of periodic or chronic intoxication produced by repeated use/consumption of a drug(natural or synthetics) a critical psychological dependence of the effects of the drug(where the user is helpless without the drug)
