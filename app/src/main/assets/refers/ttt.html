Three main groups of antiretroviral

  • Nucleoside reverse transcriptase inhibitors 158 (NRTIs),
  • Non-nucleoside reverse transcriptase inhibitors (NNRTIs) and
  • Protease inhibitors (PIs).

Each group of drugs works at a different point in the life cycle of the virus within the cell.
